PaperTrail Expiry Desk Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes the current App Store version of PaperTrail Expiry Desk.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Summary

  • PaperTrail Expiry Desk does not collect personal data.
  • PaperTrail Expiry Desk does not track users.
  • The app works locally on the user's device.
  • The app has no account system.
  • The app has no ads.
  • The app has no backend service or cloud sync.
  • The app does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

No Data Collection

PaperTrail Expiry Desk does not collect personal data from the app. The developer does not receive user-created records, app usage data, identifiers, diagnostics, or location data from the app.

No Tracking

The app does not track users across apps or websites and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

Local Storage

User records are saved on the local device. These records may include manually entered document names, expiry dates, storage notes, renewal steps, and related notes.

Export previews are generated locally on the device from the user's local records.

No Sensitive Permissions

The app does not use Location, Camera, Microphone, Contacts, Bluetooth, or tracking permissions.

No Third-Party Analytics Or Ads

The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, attribution SDKs, or backend service SDKs.

Deleting App Data

Users can delete records inside the app where available, or delete the app from the device to remove its local app data. Device backups are controlled by the user's device and Apple account settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: ingsupport@163.com.
