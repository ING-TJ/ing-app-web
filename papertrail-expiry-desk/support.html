PaperTrail Expiry Desk Support

PaperTrail Expiry Desk is a local offline iPhone utility for manually keeping document expiry dates, storage notes, and renewal steps in one place.

Last updated: June 22, 2026

App Facts

  • The app works locally on the user's device.
  • No account is required.
  • The app has no ads.
  • The app has no backend service or cloud sync.
  • User records are saved on the local device.
  • Export previews are generated locally on the device.
  • The app does not use Location, Camera, Microphone, Contacts, Bluetooth, or tracking permissions.

FAQ

Where are my records stored?

User records are stored locally on your device. PaperTrail Expiry Desk does not provide an account system, backend service, or cloud sync.

Does the app collect personal data?

No. PaperTrail Expiry Desk does not collect personal data from the app.

Does the app use ads, analytics, or tracking?

No. The app does not include advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Does the app request sensitive permissions?

No. The app does not request Location, Camera, Microphone, Contacts, Bluetooth, or App Tracking Transparency permissions.

How does export preview work?

The export preview is generated locally from records stored on the device. It is meant for manual review by the user.

How can I delete app data?

You can delete records inside the app where available, or remove the app from your device to delete its local app data.
