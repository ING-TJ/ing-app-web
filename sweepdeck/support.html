Offline digital cleanup tracking for iPhone and iPad.

About SweepDeck

SweepDeck is an offline utility app that helps you plan cleanup sessions, track digital clutter, and keep local notes about your next actions.

Account And Connectivity

SweepDeck does not require an account, login, backend server, or internet connection for its core features.

Data Storage

Your SweepDeck records are stored only on your device. The app does not collect personal data, use advertising, or use third-party analytics.

Common Questions

  • Does SweepDeck sync data? No. This version is local-only.
  • Does SweepDeck use ads? No. The app does not use advertising.
  • Does SweepDeck collect analytics? No. The app does not use third-party analytics.
  • Can I remove my data? Yes. You can clear local records inside the app, and deleting the app removes app-local data according to normal iOS behavior.
