SweepDeck Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Overview

SweepDeck is an offline utility app for planning and tracking digital cleanup sessions. The app does not require an account and does not connect to a backend server.

Data Collection

SweepDeck does not collect personal data from users.

Local Data

Records that you create in SweepDeck are stored only on your device. This may include cleanup titles, categories, dates, item counts, time spent, friction ratings, next actions, and notes that you enter.

No Accounts Or Backend

SweepDeck does not include account registration, login, cloud sync, or server-side storage.

No Advertising Or Analytics

SweepDeck does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, or tracking technologies.

Device Permissions

SweepDeck does not request access to camera, photos, contacts, calendar, location, microphone, Bluetooth, notifications, or tracking permissions.

Data Deletion

You can delete your local records inside the app. Deleting the app also removes app-local data according to normal iOS behavior.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at ingsupport@163.com.
